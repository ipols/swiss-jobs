AI Exposure of Jobs in Switzerland

This tool visualizes 406 occupations covering 3.9 million jobs across the Swiss economy. Each rectangle is one occupation — its area is proportional to the number of jobs, its color shows AI exposure, scored from 0 (green, minimal) to 10 (red, maximum). Hover any tile for details.

Each occupation was scored by a large language model (Claude) analyzing the specific tasks and skills it requires. Employment data comes from the Swiss Federal Statistical Office (BFS); occupation descriptions from the European ESCO classification. Scores estimate how much of the core work current AI can perform or accelerate.

This is a map of exposure, not a map of risk. High-scoring occupations are being reshaped by AI — that is not the same as being replaced.
